WORK

The Automatic Annotation of Piano Fingering Based on HMM
Research Assistant, Tianjin University

  • Researched several mainstream methods in automatic decision of piano fingering, including dynamic programming and Hidden Markov Model.

  • Developed a method to automatically annotate the fingering for both hands and applied it to both single note and chords.

  • Built and trained a model based on the hidden Markov model, and implemented Viterbi algorithm with the model parameters to create the most appropriate piano fingering sequence for piano players to refer to.
  • Used 5-fold cross validation and obtained the average accuracy of 57.84%.
